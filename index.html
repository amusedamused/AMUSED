Artist Statement

Hi, this Marie Dahlén, the artist behind the persona AMUSED. I’m a multidisciplinary artist that started in the game industry 13 years ago and have since un-learned and re-learned to find new ways to create and express myself. What drives my motivation for being an artist is grounded in the researching qualities of my artistic methods by gaining new knowledge in both technical aspects as well as introspective growth. This process becomes the seeds to my creations.

Since last year I've been part of the art collective 10 Percent Human. It’s a big part of how I now look at my practice as a shared process and the reflexivity that comes with co-working with a cluster of other artists. I strongly believe in sharing and learning together, encouraging and supporting each other. No human is an island so I don’t see my creations as something I created alone but as part of a wider network of friends and co-workers in the art collective and beyond.

After years of working as a 3D artist I realised that it's too restrictive to me to work with only one medium.This drive for multidisciplinary has led me to branch out from traditional painting into 3D, sound production, programming, pole dancing and lately into virtual realities and the metaverse. I’m an enthusiast of new edge technologies because I believe it’s important to be part of these new emerging platforms to contribute and make space for diversity.
